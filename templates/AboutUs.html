Why Teporingo?

The Teporingo is an endemic and endangered species of Rabbit in Mexico, although there is info about it, sometimes it's wrong or mainly made to profit from it. I wanted to give the teporingo a space where we could raise global awareness about it.

History Behind

Teporingo-bunnyadvice.com was forged from the love and care I've experienced over the last ten years, being a bunny owner, there are a lot of info and places where they talk about and cover bunny care, health, and worries but when the idea of a website hit me, I also remembered a little friend I wanted to see and admired much more on my last visit to the Zoo, the Teporingo or the Rabbit of the Volcanos

Meet Cake & Hades

The two main reasons the Teporingo was at the top of my head on my last visit to the Zoo. Cake was a black bunny my sister brought home, she was a rescued and sheltered bunny as she had marks on her ears indicating that she was rescued. She was a fierce bunny, fought against bunny cancer on her breasts and won, but at the long age of 9 years she developed a spinal disease, we took Care of her with love until her last breath, quite literally I sang her the last night she spends with us and pet her in her last breath, but as she was getting older we decided to adopt a little bunny to make up for her disease, that's were Hades came from, the mighty god of the Inframundo, Hades had quite a personality that showed up since he was a little spring, after Cake departure, I and my mom took care of Hades as also my sister departure but from home to another state, now Hades it's a big chunky boy that it's treated like a king in my house, or might I say a god?
