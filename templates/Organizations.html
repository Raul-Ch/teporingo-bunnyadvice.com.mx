TEPORINGO AMCELA

The Mexican Association for the Conservation and Study of Lagomorphs A.C. (AMCELA), is a civil association legally formed since August 21, 1990. It is made up of researchers, professionals and students as well as people interested in the ecology and conservation of rabbits. and Mexican hares

  • Promote scientific research on Mexican rabbits and hares.
  • Promote the conservation of species that are in danger of extinction, as well as their habitat.
  • Carry out environmental education activities aimed at protecting the habitat of these species

CONANP

The National Commission of Protected Natural Areas (Conanp) contributes to the preservation and sustainability of ecosystems and natural environments, representative of the biological diversity of Mexico, through the planning, management and effective, equitable, honest and transparent administration of the Mexican system of Natural Areas Protected.

Contribute to the conservation of zacatuche in the wild, through identification and execution of conservation actions in habitat and populations.

  • Reduce the factors that affect the habitat conditions and populations of the zacatuche rabbit through protection, surveillance and correct actions application of the legal framework.
  • Carry out management activities for the species and its habitat, to contribute to its recovery and conservation
  • Improve the habitat conditions of the zacatuche through the application of restoration strategies
  • Increase the participation of the various sectors of Mexican society in actions for the protection, management and recovery of the species and its habitat.

House Rabbit Society (HRS)

House Rabbit Society (HRS) is an international nonprofit animal welfare organization with two primary goals:

  • To rescue abandoned rabbits and find permanent homes for them es.
  • Through education, seek to reduce the number of unwanted rabbits — and to improve bunnies lives — by helping people better understand these often misunderstood companion animals.

Bunny C.A.R.E.

Bunny C.A.R.E. It is a space created to encourage the adoption of rabbits, and raise awareness to avoid their purchase. This body seeks to be a support and guide for people in the pre- and post-adoption process, and thus achieve a definitive and comfortable home for them.
